# Day 9 - When Plans Change, Allah Remains

## Arrival moment

Pause gently.
A changed plan does not mean a failed life.

"Perhaps you dislike something while it is good for you."

## Opening reflection

Disappointment can quietly shake faith.
But faith is not built on everything going as expected.
It is built on trusting the One who sees what you cannot yet see.

## Seerah moment

The Treaty of Hudaybiyyah looked like loss to many companions in the moment.
Yet Allah opened through it what was not visible at first sight.

Sometimes what feels like a closed door is preserved mercy.

## Quran reflection

Read with patience.

2:216
8:30

## Tafsir support

The Quran repeatedly teaches: human sight is partial, divine wisdom is complete.
Delay, redirection, and detours can carry hidden openings.

## Hadith connection

The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "How wonderful is the affair of the believer... all of his affair is good for him."

## Private reflection

What disappointment still feels unresolved in you?
How might trust reshape the way you hold it?

## Tiny action

Write one sentence: "Ya Allah, help me trust You even when I do not understand yet."

## Closing moment

Your map changed, but your Lord did not.
Walk the next step with trust.
