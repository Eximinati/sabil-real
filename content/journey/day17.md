# Day 17 - Purify Your Intention

## Arrival moment

Pause before action.
Direction in the heart matters.

"They were only commanded to worship Allah, sincere to Him in religion."

## Opening reflection

Outward good can lose its light when inward intention is scattered.
Ikhlas does not demand perfect feelings.
It asks: "For whom am I doing this?"

## Seerah moment

The Prophet (peace be upon him) taught quietly hidden deeds and sincerity away from applause.
His greatest acts were anchored in seeking Allah, not human recognition.

## Quran reflection

Read and check your intention.

98:5
76:9

## Tafsir support

Sincere intention turns ordinary acts into worship.
Showy intention can empty even religious acts of spiritual life.

## Hadith connection

The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "Actions are only by intentions."

## Private reflection

Where are you most tempted to seek validation from people?
What act today can you do quietly for Allah alone?

## Tiny action

Do one good deed today that no one knows about.

## Closing moment

When intention is purified, even small acts become luminous.
Keep your heart facing Allah.
