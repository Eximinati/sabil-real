# Day 7 - Keep Walking Gently

## Arrival moment

Take calm breath.
You do not need to rush your way to Allah.

"So be steadfast as you have been commanded."

## Opening reflection

By now you may still feel unfinished, and that is okay.
Path to Allah is not built on dramatic bursts.
It is built on sincere returning, again and again.

## Seerah moment

When revelation paused for period, Prophet (peace be upon him) felt deep human heaviness.
Then Allah revealed Surah Ad-Duha with reassurance: your Lord has not abandoned you.

Lesson is tender and timeless:
pause does not mean rejection.

## Quran reflection

Read gently and receive reassurance.

93:3
93:4
93:5

## Tafsir support

Steadiness in Islam is not perfection.
It is loyal direction.
Even small consistent acts become beloved when they come from sincerity.

## Hadith connection

Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "Most beloved deeds to Allah are those done consistently, even if small."

## Private reflection

Which small practice from this week helped your heart most?
What would make it easier to continue next week without pressure?

## Tiny action

Choose one tiny daily act (two-minute dua, one page Quran, or one dhikr moment) and commit to it for next 7 days.

## Closing moment

You are still on path.
Keep walking gently toward Allah.
Tomorrow is another mercy.
