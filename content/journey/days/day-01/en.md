# Day 1 - Allah Sees You

## Arrival moment

Take one quiet breath.
Before you explain yourself, Allah already knows your heart.

"Your Lord has not left you, nor has He turned away from you."

## Opening reflection

Many of us come to faith carrying private heaviness.
Sometimes we think we need to fix everything before turning to Allah.
Today begins with gentler truth: you are already seen with mercy.

## Seerah moment

Before revelation began, Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) would withdraw to Cave Hira.
Away from noise, he sat in stillness with sincere searching.
Then came first command: Read.
Guidance came as mercy, not as pressure.

## Tafsir support

Allah begins with "Rabb" - Lord who nurtures over time.
Learning is sacred and gradual.
You do not need to become someone else overnight.
Even confusion can become doorway to closeness when brought honestly to Allah.

## Hadith connection

Allah says in hadith qudsi: "I am as My servant expects Me to be, and I am with him when he remembers Me."

## Private reflection

What burden have you been carrying silently?
If you write directly to Allah tonight, what would your heart say first?

## Tiny action

Tonight, take two quiet minutes and speak to Allah in your own words.
No script. No performance. Just honesty.

## Closing moment

O Allah, You are near, You see me, and You know me.
Keep my heart sincere, soften what is heavy, and bring me closer with mercy.
Return tomorrow gently.
