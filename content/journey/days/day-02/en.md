# Day 2 - You Were Created With Purpose

## Arrival moment

Take one quiet breath.
You are not here by accident.

"I did not create jinn and mankind except to worship Me."

## Opening reflection

Many of us move through our days in survival mode - reacting, performing, and carrying more than we can name.
Islam begins by restoring direction.
Your life is not random.
Your heart was created with a qiblah.

## Seerah moment

In Makkah, when Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) stood at Mount Safa and called his people, he invited them first to truth before status.
Not to tribe. Not to pride. Not to performance.
He called them back to Allah.

This call was costly, but it was clear: life becomes coherent when Allah is at center.

## Quran reflection

Sit with these verses slowly.

51:56
67:2

## Tafsir support

These verses do not reduce life to ritual tasks.
"Worship" here means living in conscious relationship with Allah - in prayer, work, family life, and private moments.
Test of life is not who appears most successful, but whose heart becomes more sincere.

## Hadith connection

Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "Most beloved deeds to Allah are those done regularly, even if small."

## Private reflection

Where in your life have you been living on autopilot?
Where do you feel most disconnected from your deeper purpose?

## Tiny action

Tonight, before sleep, whisper this dua in your own words:
"Allah, help me live tomorrow with You at center."

## Closing moment

Purpose is not discovered in one dramatic moment.
It is uncovered every time you turn your heart back to Allah.
Return tomorrow gently.
