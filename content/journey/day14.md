# Day 14 - Trust After Delay

## Arrival moment

Pause gently.
Delay is not abandonment.

"Perhaps your Lord will give you, and you will be satisfied."

## Opening reflection

Delayed answers can feel heavy.
But delay can be part of divine care.
Allah is not late.
Allah is wise.

## Seerah moment

There were moments when revelation paused, and the heart felt the weight of waiting.
Then Allah sent reassurance with tenderness.

Waiting became a place of trust.

## Quran reflection

Read with patience and hope.

93:5
2:214

## Tafsir support

A believer's waiting is not empty.
It can deepen sincerity, purify dependence, and prepare the heart for what is better timed.

## Hadith connection

The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "Know that victory comes with patience, relief with affliction, and ease with hardship."

## Private reflection

Where are you most impatient with Allah's timing?
What helps you remain faithful while waiting?

## Tiny action

Choose one unresolved matter and entrust it to Allah in dua for seven days without despair language.

## Closing moment

Keep trusting in the waiting.
Allah's timing carries mercy you may not see yet.
