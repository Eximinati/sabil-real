# Day 21 - Courageous Truthfulness

## Arrival moment

Take a steady breath.
Truth can be gentle and brave at once.

"O you who believe, fear Allah and be with the truthful."

## Opening reflection

Truthfulness is not only about avoiding lies.
It is about integrity between what is in your heart, on your tongue, and in your actions.

## Seerah moment

The Prophet (peace be upon him) was known as Al-Amin before prophethood.
Even his opponents trusted his honesty.
Truthfulness built credibility before da'wah expanded.

## Quran reflection

Read with intention.

9:119
33:24

## Tafsir support

Sidq is a lifelong discipline.
It includes truthful speech, truthful intentions, and truthful commitments.
Truth anchors faith.

## Hadith connection

The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "Truthfulness leads to righteousness, and righteousness leads to Paradise."

## Private reflection

Where are you tempted to hide, exaggerate, or avoid truth?
What one truthful step can you take today with wisdom and kindness?

## Tiny action

Speak one needed truth today gently, without harshness or blame.

## Closing moment

Truthfulness may feel difficult in the moment,
but it brings deep peace to the heart.
