# Day 23 - Let Quran Become a Companion

## Arrival moment

Sit quietly.
Read to be met, not to finish.

"This Quran guides to what is most upright."

## Opening reflection

A companion is someone you return to repeatedly.
The Quran becomes companionship when recitation turns into relationship.

## Seerah moment

The Prophet (peace be upon him) recited Quran in hardship and ease.
Its words were not occasional inspiration - they were daily nourishment.

## Quran reflection

Read these verses slowly.

17:9
10:57

## Tafsir support

Guidance from Quran deepens with repetition and reflection.
Even short daily recitation can transform inner life when done with presence.

## Hadith connection

The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "The example of one who remembers his Lord and one who does not is like the living and the dead."

## Private reflection

What makes Quran reading feel difficult for you lately?
What would a compassionate daily rhythm look like?

## Tiny action

Read one page today and pause after each verse for one breath.

## Closing moment

You do not need large volume to begin.
You need a sincere return.
