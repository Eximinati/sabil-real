# Day 10 - Allah Is Enough For You

## Arrival moment

Take a still breath.
You are not held up by your strength alone.

"Is Allah not sufficient for His servant?"

## Opening reflection

Fear often says: "I need one more guarantee before I can rest."
Faith says: "Allah is enough, even when guarantees are absent."

## Seerah moment

In vulnerable moments, the Prophet (peace be upon him) never severed his dependence on Allah.
Even with companions around him, his deepest reliance remained with his Lord.

## Quran reflection

Read and pause.

39:36
9:129

## Tafsir support

"Allah is enough" does not remove responsibility.
It removes inner collapse.
It gives the heart a place to stand when means look small.

## Hadith connection

The Prophet (peace be upon him) would often say: "O Ever-Living, O Sustainer, by Your mercy I seek relief."

## Private reflection

In what area do you feel unsupported right now?
What changes when you remember Allah's sufficiency there?

## Tiny action

Repeat quietly 10 times today: "Hasbiyallahu wa ni'mal wakeel."

## Closing moment

You are not unsupported.
You are carried by the One who does not fail.
