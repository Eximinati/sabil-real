# Day 29 - If You Missed Days, Return Gently

## Arrival moment

Welcome back.
You are not behind in a race.

"And do not despair of the mercy of Allah."

## Opening reflection

Missing days happens.
Life gets heavy.
The Sabil path is built on return, not perfection.

## Seerah moment

The Prophet (peace be upon him) taught people to keep returning to Allah after slips and pauses.
He did not build faith on humiliation; he built it on mercy and responsibility.

## Quran reflection

Read as someone being welcomed back.

39:53
2:222

## Tafsir support

A missed day is not spiritual collapse.
It is an invitation to renew intention with humility and hope.

## Hadith connection

The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "All children of Adam err, and the best of those who err are those who repent."

## Private reflection

What made returning difficult for you recently?
What compassionate structure could help you return more gently next time?

## Tiny action

Restart with one small act tonight - even two sincere lines of dua.

## Closing moment

You can always begin again with Allah.
Return gently, without shame.
