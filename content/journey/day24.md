# Day 24 - Mercy in Relationships

## Arrival moment

Take a calming breath.
Faith is seen in how mercy appears in your relationships.

"And We have not sent you except as a mercy to the worlds."

## Opening reflection

Worship is not only private devotion.
It also appears in tone, patience, and kindness with people near you.

## Seerah moment

The Prophet (peace be upon him) showed gentleness in his home, patience with those who offended him, and care for the vulnerable.
His mercy was practical and daily.

## Quran reflection

Read and reflect on conduct.

21:107
3:159

## Tafsir support

Mercy is strength with softness.
It includes forgiveness, wise boundaries, and refusing cruelty.

## Hadith connection

The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "The merciful are shown mercy by the Most Merciful."

## Private reflection

Where do you need more mercy in your speech or reactions?
What relationship most needs gentleness from you now?

## Tiny action

Offer one intentional act of kindness today to someone in your daily circle.

## Closing moment

Mercy is one of the clearest signs of living faith.
Carry it into your next interaction.
