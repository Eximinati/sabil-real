# Day 22 - Prayer as Your Daily Anchor

## Arrival moment

Pause and remember.
Salah is not an interruption of life - it is its anchor.

"Seek help through patience and prayer."

## Opening reflection

When days feel scattered, prayer recenters the heart.
Salah gives your soul recurring appointments with Allah.

## Seerah moment

Even in hardship, the Prophet (peace be upon him) returned to prayer for relief and grounding.
Salah was his refuge, not a burden.

## Quran reflection

Read and sit with these verses.

2:45
29:45

## Tafsir support

Prayer protects over time.
Its effect is often subtle but steady - softening impulses and restoring awareness.

## Hadith connection

The Prophet (peace be upon him) said to Bilal: "Give us comfort with it, O Bilal" (referring to prayer).

## Private reflection

Which prayer is hardest for you to hold consistently?
What small support could make it easier this week?

## Tiny action

Choose one prayer today to protect with extra care and calm.

## Closing moment

Your prayer is a returning point in every day.
Return there gently.
