# Day 15 - Begin With Honest Self-Seeing

## Arrival moment

Sit quietly for a moment.
Honesty with Allah begins with honesty about yourself.

"O you who believe, fear Allah, and let every soul look to what it has sent ahead for tomorrow."

## Opening reflection

Growth starts when defensiveness softens.
Muhasabah is not self-hatred.
It is truthful seeing so your heart can return with clarity.

## Seerah moment

The Prophet (peace be upon him) made regular istighfar despite being the most righteous.
This was not because he was spiritually insecure.
It was because his heart lived in continual humility before Allah.

## Quran reflection

Read and sit with yourself.

59:18
91:9

## Tafsir support

Self-accounting in Islam is protective mercy.
It helps prevent hidden drift before it becomes open harm.

## Hadith connection

The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "The wise one is the one who calls himself to account and works for what comes after death."

## Private reflection

What pattern in you needs honest naming right now?
What one change feels sincere and possible this week?

## Tiny action

Write one sentence tonight: "Ya Allah, help me face this truth with mercy and courage."

## Closing moment

Honesty is not the end of hope.
It is the beginning of real return.
