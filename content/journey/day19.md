# Day 19 - Heal Comparison and Envy

## Arrival moment

Pause and return inward.
Your path with Allah is not a competition.

"Do not wish for what Allah has given some of you over others."

## Opening reflection

Comparison quietly steals peace.
It makes blessings look small and hearts grow restless.
Islam teaches contentment, effort, and prayer for others.

## Seerah moment

The Prophet (peace be upon him) nurtured hearts away from rivalry and toward brotherhood.
He taught believers to love good for each other, not resent it.

## Quran reflection

Read and release envy gently.

4:32
113:5

## Tafsir support

Hasad harms the one who carries it before harming anyone else.
A cure begins with gratitude, dua for others, and asking Allah for halal increase.

## Hadith connection

The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "Do not envy one another."

## Private reflection

Where does comparison show up in your life most?
How can you replace envy with dua and gratitude there?

## Tiny action

Make one sincere dua for someone whose blessing has been difficult for you to accept.

## Closing moment

What Allah gives others does not reduce what He can give you.
Return to trust and gratitude.
